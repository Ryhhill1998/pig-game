PIG GAME

HOW TO PLAY

Roll the dice to start the game.


If neither die shows a score of 1, the total score is added to the active player's currect score.


The active player can then click hold to save the current score to their score. Play switches players.


If either die shows a score of 1, current score is set to 0. Play switches players.


If both dice show a score of 1, the active player's score is reset to 0. Play switches players.


The first player to reach a score of 100 wins!
